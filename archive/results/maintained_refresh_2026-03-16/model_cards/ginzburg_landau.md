# Ginzburg-Landau Model Card

## Mathematical Model

$J(u)=\int_\Omega \frac{\varepsilon}{2}\lvert\nabla u\rvert^2 + \frac{1}{4}(u^2-1)^2\,dx$ with $\varepsilon=0.01$ and homogeneous Dirichlet conditions on $[-1,1]^2$.

## Geometry And Setup

Non-convex scalar phase-field benchmark with indefinite Hessian behavior near the wells.

## Discretization And Mesh Source

P1 Lagrange FE on structured triangular meshes generated by the maintained scalar mesh loaders.
